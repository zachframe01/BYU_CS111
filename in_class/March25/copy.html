Syllabus & Course Policies

Overview

In CS 111, we are interested in teaching you about programming, not about how to use a specific programming language. We consider a series of techniques for controlling program complexity, such as functional programming, data abstraction, and object-oriented programming.

  1. CS 110 focuses on learning how to program for beginners.
  2. CS 111 concentrates on the idea of abstraction.
    • It teaches advanced Python programming.
    • It allows the programmer to think in terms appropriate to the problem rather than in low-level operations.
  3. More information may be found on the CS 111 website.
